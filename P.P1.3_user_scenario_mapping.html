Fatima Dirie: CustomCtrl User Scenario Mapping

Scenario

Stella recently graduated from college and began working at her new part time job. Now that she is making a better salary, she wants to begin investing in her hobbies more and start decorating her room. She loves playing video games on her pc, but has trouble finding high quality tech that looks aethetically pleasing. She doesn't like any of the preset colors leading tech brands offer, and wants a very specific color theme in her room. Stella;s friend tells her about CustomCtrl.com, and she is quick to customize her keyboard,headsets, mouse, and mousepad all in the color scheme she wants. Now, she is closer to her dream gaming set up. She was even able to have her own name printed on some of the equipment.

1. Actor

Stella Johnson

2. Motivator

Can now afford to invest in her dream gaming setup.

3. Intention/intent

Stella needs to be able to find the specific colors of tech she needs in high quality equipment.

4. Action

Stella picks her equipment and color choices on CustomCtrl.com, and adds her name to be printed on the equipment.

5. Resolution

Stella has her dream gaming setup.
